How it works & limitations
  • It extracts every embedded image from each PDF and compares it to your reference using a normalized grayscale correlation score (robust to scaling and re-encoding).
  • Works when the signature is embedded as its own image (JPEG, or uncompressed/Flate). It does not decode JPEG2000, JBIG2 or CCITT-fax images, and it cannot find a signature that is only part of a larger flattened page image, or one drawn as vector strokes.
  • Use the thumbnail and score to confirm — lower the threshold if a known signature is just under the line.
